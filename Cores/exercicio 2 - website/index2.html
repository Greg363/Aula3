Which Salad is the Best in the World?

While salads are objectively the best food ever created. We are still left to determine the best salad in the world. Caesar salad, Coleslaw, Chicken salad... Which one is the best?! Well, we are about to find that out today.

Garden salad

Your classic and common salad found worldwide. Made with lettuce such as iceberg, romaine or mesclun greens and including toppings such as tomatoes, carrots, onions, cucumbers, mushrooms and bell peppers. We have all been forced to eat a salad like this one before! Its healthy, its boring and it can taste surprisingly good! And as such, the Garden salad wins 5th place.

Ramen salad

Originating from Hokkaido, Japan, in 4th place we have the Ramen salad! Made with lettuce, tomato, sweetcorn, cucumber, and egg. It is dressed with sesame dressing and Japanese mayonnaise. It is often served in Izakayas in the region as a light snack. While looking like a food abomination it does taste pretty good! Its ramen and a salad, how is that going to taste bad?

Potato salad

Found worldwide and in 3rd place we have the Potato salad. Made from potatoes, and varies throughout different regions of the world. American versions often use mayonnaise, sour cream or milk dressing. We all love potatoes, so placing them into a salad should make us love salads... right..? Well, probably not. But we love potato salads so 3rd place it is!

Caprese salad

Originating from the italian region of Campania we have the Caprese salad. Made of sliced fresh buffalo mozzarella, tomatoes and basil, and seasoned with salt, pepper, and olive oil. This salad is extremely good, and its gorgeous to look at! We are also a bit biased because it appears in the anime Jojo's Bizzare Adventure, so well... 2nd place!

Honorable mention

Caesar salad. Originating from Mexico and made using Romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with parmesan cheese, lemon juice, olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, and black pepper. We all have gone to the popular fast food company McDonalds before, seen their Caesar salad and said "Why would anyone eat that?" Well, to those people all we have to say is you should try it some time, it might taste better than you expect!

Pasta salad

Pasta salad, objectively, the best salad ever made. Prepared with one or more types of pasta, usually chilled, and most often tossed in a vinegar, oil or mayonnaise-based dressing. It looks good, tastes good and makes us want more and more. First place, thats all we have to say.
